RESEARCH STUDIES


6 in 10 Singaporeans eat out at least 4 times a week





Regularly eat out consume 15% more calories and 25% more saturated fat!

which contributes to 3 in 10 Singaporeans diagnose with diabetes before their 40th birthday





With 1 in 5 Singaporeans working more than 11 hours a day, they have little time and energy after spending long hours at work to cook for themselves

EMPATHY

CONTEXTUAL INQUIRY

31 Respondents

13 Homemakers and 21 Working Adults who are corresponded to our target user archetype

Survey + Interviews

At least 10 minutes face-to-face interviews and online surveys were conducted.

Field Observation

20 to 30 minutes observing housemakers prepare dishes for their family, while the working adults carry out activities at home after work. Share their experiences during their activities.







Results + Findings

Homemakers

53.8% respondents would like to earn extra income by selling own home-cooked food to neighbourhood community!

Working Adults

Majority would like to try healthier and cheaper food which is cooked by your neighbouring certified cook!



“I love to produce confectioneries for my colleagues and it would be great to earn extra income on the side."

Employee & Homemaker, age 45



“If the using experience of the first time is good, I will keep on trying.”

Fresh Graduate, Young Working Adult, age 24

Our Solution

SOLUTION


Reunion is an app which allows you to order delicious food from certified home cooks in your neighborhood community. Busy working adults can purchase home-cooked meals without the hassle of shopping, cooking and cleaning.

At the same time, this platform brings in opportunities for homemakers to earn extra income simply through making eatra portions of what they have cooked.
